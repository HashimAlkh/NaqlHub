## 3. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use NaqlHub, we may collect the following information.

### Account Information

We may collect your name, email address, password, phone number, preferred language, and account settings.

Passwords are managed securely through Supabase Authentication and are not stored by NaqlHub in plain text.

### Profile Information

We may collect your company name, role, contact preferences, vehicle information if provided, and job alert preferences.

### Job Listings

When creating a transport request, we may store origin, destination, cargo type, vehicle type, weight, dimensions, budget, description, contact information, and uploaded images if supported.

### Job Alerts

When creating Job Alerts, we may store your selected routes, cargo preferences, vehicle preferences, and notification settings.

Job Alerts may be used to send matching transport opportunities through email, WhatsApp, or other channels selected by the user.

### Technical Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information, including IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, log files, authentication events, and security-related data.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- provide and operate the platform;
- create and manage user accounts;
- authenticate users;
- publish and manage transport listings;
- deliver job alerts;
- enable users to contact each other;
- improve platform performance and user experience;
- maintain security and prevent abuse;
- send transactional emails;
- respond to support requests;
- comply with legal obligations.

## 5. Job Alerts and Notifications

If you enable Job Alerts, we process your selected preferences to notify you when matching transport requests become available.

Notifications may be delivered by email, WhatsApp, or other communication channels introduced in the future.

You may disable or delete Job Alerts at any time.

## 6. Communications Between Users

When you publish a transport listing, certain contact information may become visible to other users to enable communication regarding that listing.

Users are responsible for any communication that takes place outside the NaqlHub platform, including communication through WhatsApp, telephone, email, or other third-party services.

## 7. Cookies

NaqlHub uses cookies and similar technologies for essential platform functionality.

These cookies may be used for authentication, maintaining secure sessions, remembering user preferences, protecting accounts, and preventing abuse.

We do not currently use advertising cookies.

If analytics or marketing cookies are introduced in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

## 8. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to operate NaqlHub.

### Supabase

Supabase is used for user authentication, database storage, account management, and secure session handling.

### Vercel

Vercel may be used for hosting and delivering the website.

### Resend

Resend is used for transactional emails, including account verification, password reset emails, and important account notifications.

### Meta / WhatsApp

If WhatsApp notifications are enabled, phone numbers and message-related information required for message delivery may be processed through Meta's WhatsApp Cloud API.
